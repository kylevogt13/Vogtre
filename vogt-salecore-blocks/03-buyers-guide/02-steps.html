1

Analyze Your Situation

Determine readiness to buy by discussing your goals and needs. Communication is essential throughout the journey to home ownership.

2

Finance & Understand the Numbers

Organize finances with help from recommended lenders. Credit review, determine affordable price ranges, and obtain formal pre-approval.

3

Shop

Create a wish list of desired home and community features. Your agent matches priorities with budget constraints using local expertise.

4

Offer & Negotiate

Submit a data-driven offer based on comparative market analysis. Negotiate contingencies and contract terms effectively.

5

Offer Acceptance

Once accepted, title and escrow processes begin, typically lasting 17-25 days before closing.

6

Title and Escrow

Title company handles closing and researches property title, liens, and ownership chain. Transaction timeline typically 17-25 days.

7

Seller Disclosures, Lender Documents & Appraisal

Review seller disclosures, sign loan documents, and order appraisals (approximately $600-$800).

8

Inspections

Schedule property inspections within days of offer acceptance. Inspections cost about $600 and up.

9

Final Loan Approval & Signing

Sign final documents and closing disclosure outlining all costs approximately one week before closing.

10

Celebrate!

Collect keys after title confirms close of escrow. Contact utility companies and update mailing address.
